About Me

I am a dedicated and innovative developer passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

My journey with the Digital Evaluation Platform stems from my role as the Head of the Mathematics/ICT department at a renowned school, Brilliant Footsteps International Academy, located in Sokoto State. Witnessing the challenges and inefficiencies associated with manual evaluation processes, I was driven to develop a solution that would not only simplify marking and result generation but also align with the digital demands of the 21st century educational landscape.

This platform is our solution to providing a seamless user experience for digital assessments.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. Learn more about Holberton School.
